Alcohol & Drug Laws

BAC Limits

  • 0.08% BAC = illegal for drivers 21+
  • 0.04% BAC = commercial drivers
  • Any detectable alcohol = under 21 in most states

Penalties

  • License suspension and fines
  • Possible jail time for repeat offenses
  • SR-22 insurance requirement after DUI conviction

Drugs & Medication

  • Prescription drugs can impair driving.
  • Medical marijuana rules vary by state.
  • Field sobriety tests check impairment, not just alcohol.
Warning: Even one drink can impair reaction time. If you feel any effect, do not drive.
