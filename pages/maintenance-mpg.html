Fuel Economy Tips

Driving Habits

  • Drive smoothly and avoid hard acceleration.
  • Use cruise control on steady highways.
  • Reduce idling — turn off engine for longer stops.
  • Observe speed limits; aerodynamics worsen at high speed.

Maintenance

  • Keep tires inflated to recommended PSI.
  • Remove roof racks or roof boxes when unused.
  • Stay current on oil and air-filter service.
  • Fix check-engine issues promptly.
